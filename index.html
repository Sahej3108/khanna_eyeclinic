Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment at Khanna Eye Center, Nirman Vihar?

You can fill out the contact form or visit us for a walk-in appointment. However, walk-ins are subject to availability. We advise you to call or book an appointment before visiting.

How long does it take for my eye checkup?

A routine eye checkup typically takes 30–60 minutes, depending on your eye condition and the tests required. More comprehensive evaluations may take longer.

What is the cost of an eye specialist consultation at Khanna Eye Center?

Consultation charges may vary based on the specialist and the type of evaluation required. Please contact the centre directly for the latest consultation fees and appointment details.

What eye treatment does Khanna Eye Center offer?

Khanna Eye Center offers comprehensive eye care services, including LASIK, cataract surgery, retina treatment, glaucoma management, cornea care, pediatric ophthalmology, oculoplasty, and more.

Does Khanna Eye Center offer teleconsultations or video appointments?

Teleconsultation availability may vary depending on the nature of your eye condition. Please contact our team to check eligibility and schedule a virtual consultation.

What are the Khanna Eye Center timings?

Khanna Eye Center is open from 10:00 AM to 7:30 PM, providing convenient access to consultations, diagnostic services, and specialized eye treatments throughout the day.

Does Khanna Eye Center accept health insurance?

Yes, Khanna Eye Center accepts health insurance and reimbursements with leading providers.

Does Khanna Eye Center provide insurance claim support?

Yes, our team assists patients with insurance-related documentation and claim procedures, helping ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

What latest technologies does Khanna Eye Center use?

Khanna Eye Center uses advanced technologies for cataract, LASIK, retina, glaucoma, and corneal treatments, including the world's fastest tracker system and the latest sutureless Micro Vitrectomy System.

What precautions does Khanna Eye Center recommend during eye treatment?

Patients should follow prescribed medications, attend scheduled follow-ups, avoid rubbing their eyes, and adhere to all post-treatment instructions provided by their eye specialist.
